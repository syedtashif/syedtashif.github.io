About Me

Passionate about making AI transparent and trustworthy

Hello! I'm Syed Mohd Tashif

I'm a final year AI Engineering student at ZHCET, Aligarh Muslim University, with a strong passion for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Explainable AI. Currently maintaining a CGPA of 9.00, I'm dedicated to understanding not just how AI works, but why it makes the decisions it does.

My research focuses on AI Model Interpretability and Explainability, where I investigate various XAI techniques to understand transformer-based models. I believe that for AI to be truly beneficial to society, it must be transparent, interpretable, and trustworthy.

Through my projects, I've worked on multimodal learning, medical image classification, and contrast vector-based approaches, always with an emphasis on understanding model behavior and ensuring reliable performance across diverse scenarios.
